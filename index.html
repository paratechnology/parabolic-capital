What we do

We build and back practical businesses.

Parabolic Capital owns and develops operating interests in sectors where better products, better records, and better management can improve how real businesses work. The focus is simple: useful companies, clear operations, and long-term value.

01

Build from the workflow

Each company starts with a clear business problem: bookings, contracts, invoices, stock, payments, or customer records.

02

Keep the sectors clear

Legal, hospitality, commerce, business software, civic data, wellness, and events each have separate customers and priorities.

03

Operate for the long term

We prefer businesses that can improve with better systems, stronger customer trust, and disciplined execution.
