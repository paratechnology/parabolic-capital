Product case note

PhiLedger

Multi-tenant business operations software for Nigerian SMBs: invoicing, payments, inventory, procurement, sales orders, delivery notes, expenses, reports, and team access.

SectorSMB finance and operations
SurfaceFront page, web app, Android, desktop, API
StatusFront page live, platform walkthrough available

Product views

Front page, platform dashboard, and mobile product direction.

PhiLedger is more than the website: it is a tenant-scoped operating system for billing, stock, customer records, and business reporting.

PhiLedger is evidence of full product ownership across a multi-tenant platform, with business documents, stock, customers, and payments as the core model.

Front page Platform walkthrough available

What it is

PhiLedger helps small businesses move from informal records into a tenant-scoped system that tracks what was quoted, sold, paid, stocked, purchased, delivered, and reported.

What I built

  • Invoice, estimate, payment, product, contact, inventory, expense, sales-order, purchase-order, and delivery-note flows.
  • Dashboard and reporting surfaces for overdue invoices, recent activity, low stock, and operational counts.
  • Responsive app layouts across web, Android, and desktop builds.
  • Front page and release/download surfaces for the public product story.

Technical surface

PhiLedger uses Angular, Ionic-style mobile patterns, Capacitor for Android, Tauri for desktop, NestJS for the API, shared contracts, and typed models for the business records that travel between client and server.

Product judgment

SMB operations are full of small details that matter: invoice statuses, delivery notes linked to sales orders, stock movement, contact history, payment balance, and simple language for non-technical users. The product work was making those details legible.

Good walkthrough areas: the document model, mobile/desktop packaging, and keeping complex business records understandable in the interface.
