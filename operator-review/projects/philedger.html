Product case note

PhiLedger

Business operations software for Nigerian SMBs: invoicing, payments, inventory, procurement, sales orders, delivery notes, expenses, reports, and team access.

SectorSMB finance and operations
SurfaceWebsite, web app, Android, desktop, API
StatusPublic website live, product walkthrough available

PhiLedger is evidence of full product ownership across multiple platforms, with business documents and inventory as the core model.

Website App walkthrough available

What it is

PhiLedger helps small businesses move from informal records into a system that tracks what was quoted, sold, paid, stocked, purchased, delivered, and reported.

What I built

  • Invoice, estimate, payment, product, contact, inventory, expense, sales-order, purchase-order, and delivery-note flows.
  • Dashboard and reporting surfaces for overdue invoices, recent activity, low stock, and operational counts.
  • Responsive app layouts across web, Android, and desktop builds.
  • Marketing website and release/download surfaces for the public product story.

Technical surface

PhiLedger uses Angular, Ionic-style mobile patterns, Capacitor for Android, Tauri for desktop, NestJS for the API, shared contracts, and typed models for the business records that travel between client and server.

Product judgment

SMB operations are full of small details that matter: invoice statuses, delivery notes linked to sales orders, stock movement, contact history, payment balance, and simple language for non-technical users. The product work was making those details legible.

I can discuss the document model, mobile/desktop packaging, and how I kept complex business records understandable in the interface.
