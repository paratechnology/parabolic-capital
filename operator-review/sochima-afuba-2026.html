Product engineer profile

I build useful products from messy operating workflows.

I work best where the problem is still being shaped: understanding what users are trying to do, turning the workflow into software, and staying close enough to make the product useful.

Role target

Product Engineer, Full-stack Product Builder, or Product-minded Software Engineer.

Working range

Comfortable owning ambiguous workflows end-to-end, from problem framing and interface design to system boundaries and rollout.

Evidence base

Built operating products across legal infrastructure, hospitality, commerce, civic data, wellness, events, and SME systems.

How I work

I start with the job users are trying to get done.

Most of my work begins with a practical workflow that is too manual, scattered, or hard to trust. I try to understand the real task first, then decide what should be simplified, structured, automated, or left alone.

The products I have built span matters, signatures, bookings, ledgers, stock, events, civic data, fitness scheduling, and internal delivery. The common thread is turning loose operations into clear product surfaces.
