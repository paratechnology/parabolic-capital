Why Accurx

Accurx describes the kind of engineering I am trying to do more deliberately.

The roles are not framed as “just write code.” They ask for someone who owns the problem, works close to users, shapes what gets built with product and design, and makes engineering decisions with the wider system in mind.

That is the thread through my work so far. I have been building products where the real challenge is not a shiny interface, but making fragmented work easier to coordinate: matters, signatures, bookings, ledgers, stock, events, civic data, and internal delivery.
