Approach

We back companies that solve clear operating problems.

Parabolic Capital focuses on sectors where customers need better records, better booking flows, better payment visibility, better compliance, or better day-to-day management.

Core view

The idea is simple: useful companies are built around real customer needs.

Each interest starts with a concrete problem. A law firm needs better matter records. A property owner needs better booking control. A small business needs cleaner invoices, stock, and payment records. A fitness studio needs scheduling, membership, and attendance.

Parabolic Capital invests in and builds companies where better execution can improve the product, the customer experience, the reporting, and the long-term value of the business.

01

Build from the workflow

Start with what operators already do, then reduce friction around the most repeated, valuable, and trust-sensitive work.

02

Own sector depth

Cluster interests by market function so each asset benefits from shared learning without losing its own focus.

03

Compound patiently

Prefer businesses that become more useful as they gain history, integrations, local knowledge, and customer trust.
